Much like the City of Troy...

Troy Grooming Co. is evolving with the future by remembering the past.

Continuing the tradition of the Old School barbershop while keeping pace with the lifestyle of today's man, Troy Grooming Co. is the shop you will want to visit. In addition to cuts and shaves, Troy Grooming Co. offers a line of fine, handcrafted products such as pomade, aftershave, elixirs and tonics prepared especially for men.

The neighborhood barbershop was the place to go for a fresh cut, a hot shave, and to catch up with neighbors and friends. At Troy Grooming Co., we have brought back those simple pleasures of yesterday. Stop in to visit Jamie, have an espresso, play a game of checkers, catch up on a good book from our bookshelf, or browse through a magazine from our rack.

You will like being one of our weekly visitors.
